# Jellio’s Underwater World-Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

## Overview 

Jellio is an augmented reality game designed for children ages 4+. We take your child’s privacy extremely seriously. This policy explains what data we collect (and what we don’t), how we use the camera, and your rights as a parent or guardian.

### Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your child. Jellio does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind.
We do NOT collect:
* Names, email addresses, or contact information
* Device identifiers or advertising IDs
* Location data
* Photos, videos, or images from your camera
* Usage analytics or behavioral data
* Health or diagnostic information
* Camera Access

Jellio requests access to your device’s camera solely to power its augmented reality (AR) gameplay using Apple’s ARKit framework. The camera creates an interactive AR environment on your screen.
We do not capture, save, record, or transmit any images or video from your camera. Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never stored or shared.
If you deny camera permission, AR features will not function. You can grant or revoke camera access at any time in your device’s Settings.

### Third-Party Services
Jellio does not include any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.


### Children’s Privacy (COPPA)
Jellio is designed for children ages 6–8 and complies fully with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No parental consent for data collection is required because no data is collected.

### In-App Purchases
If Jellio includes in-app purchases, a parental gate is presented before any purchase flow is initiated. This requires an adult-level action to proceed, preventing unintended purchases by children.
External Links
Any links to external websites or content (such as a support page) are placed behind a parental gate that requires a parent or guardian to complete an adult-level task before proceeding.

### Data Security
 Jellio does not collect or store any personal data, there is no personal data at risk. All AR processing occurs locally on your device using Apple’s native ARKit framework.

### Changes to This Policy
If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the revised version in the app and update the effective date above. Continued use of Jellio after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
